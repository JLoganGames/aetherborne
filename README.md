Aetherborne

Aetherborne is an action RPG focused on loot-driven progression, open-world exploration, and player-defined power growth. Players level up by completing quests, defeating enemies, and hunting for rare, tier-based gear dropped from specific mobs and bosses. Strategic stat allocation and exploration shape each player's unique path.
